# Support

For Private Singing Studio support, contact:

support [at] yixiao [dot] uk

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I send feedback from inside the App?

Yes. Open Settings and choose Send Feedback. The App will open an email draft with basic diagnostic information and a diagnostic log snapshot attached. You can edit the message and remove the attachment before sending.

### Does the App work offline?

Yes. The current version is designed to work offline on your Mac.

### Does the App upload my audio?

No. The current version does not upload your microphone input, recordings, imported songs, stems, or exported files to our servers.

### Why does the App ask for microphone access?

Microphone access is used for recording, monitoring, and singing-practice features that you choose to use. You can disable microphone access in macOS System Settings.

### Why does the App ask for file access?

File access is used only for audio files or folders that you choose to open, import, process, or export.

### How do I report a problem?

Use Settings > Send Feedback, or email the support address above with your macOS version, App version, a short description of the issue, and any steps needed to reproduce it. Do not send private audio files unless we specifically ask for a sample and you agree to share it.
